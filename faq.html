Reiki Sessions FAQ

 How can Reiki help me?

Reiki works by energetically balancing your body, thoughts, and emotions to create a deep state of relaxation.  Many clients report an enhanced sense of well-being, less stress, and better sleep.

What can I expect during a session?

I begin with a brief discussion about what concerns bring you to my office. Then, you recline fully-clothed on a massage table while I place my hands either on or above your body; sessions usually last 45 - 60 minutes. You may feel sensations of heat or tingles and experience deep relaxation. If possible, a slower pace throughout your day is recommended and extra hydration is advised.

How often should I have a session?

Everyone is different. Acute issues may require a session every few days for a brief period of time. Chronic conditions respond well over time, as sessions are cumulative in nature. We will talk about the best plan for you.
